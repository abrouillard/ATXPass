See the very best that Austin has to offer - with up to 40% savings. The three-day pass gives you access to over 21 tours and activities so that you can customize your very own itinerary. Whether it is visiting a museum, going on a food & beer tour or kayaking on Lady Bird Lake, the ATX Pass will save you money as you make the most of your time in Austin.
First, you buy the ATX Pass. You will receive a unique link with a list of activities that you can book. Once you book your activity, show up at the operator and present your booking confirmation email then you are set. You can book multiple activities over the three-day period as long as they are not at the same time or a repeat activity.
The ATX Pass gives you the ability to choose from over 21 of Austin’s favorite activities. These range from museums, bus tours and more. New activities are always added to the pass so you have an even wider choice of things to do in Austin for one low price.
    What does it cost?

    The ATX Pass is $88 for adult and $44 for kids (ages 3-12). This gives you the access to book a wide range of tours and activities in Austin.

    How will I receive my ATX Pass?

    Once you’ve purchased your ATX Pass, you’ll receive a confirmation email and SMS with a link included. Click the link to browse and choose the tours and activities you are interested in.

    How do I activate my ATX Pass?

    Once you have purchased your pass and scheduled your first activity, your pass will be automatically activated.

    How many activities are included in the pass?

    We have over 20 activities available on the pass. There are always new ones added so check our website for a complete and updated list.

    How long is the ATX Pass valid?

    Your ATX Pass will be valid for 3 days from the start date selected.

    What is the cancellation policy?

    Your pass becomes non-refundable or non-transferable once you’ve made a reservation for your first tour or activity. If you have bought the pass but have not made any reservations for tours or activities, your pass is refundable. Reach out OnceThere by calling (800)311-ONCE (6623) or support@oncethere.com to complete this process.

    Can I participate in a tour or activity more than once?

    You’ll only be able to participate in a tour or activity once with your ATX Pass. Additionally, to ensure you won’t book overlapping tours, there is a 2 hour window block after each activity that is booked.

    How do I make reservations using my pass?

    Click the link included in the booking confirmation email or the confirmation SMS. From there, you’ll be able to choose which tour and activities you like to participate in while your pass is valid.

    Can I share my pass or sell it?

    No, one pass per individual.

    What happens if the tour or activity is cancelled due to bad weather or other problems?

    Should the tour or activity be canceled by the provider, you’ll either have the opportunity to reschedule or schedule another tour or activity.

    Do you offer additional discounts for seniors, military, students or groups?

    Since this pass offers activities at a discounted rate, no additional discounts will be given.

    How long is the ATX Pass valid?

    Each pass is valid for 3 days, the validity period will begin on the day you choose to start your reservation.

    Do I receive a paper copy of the pass?

    No. Reservations for Austin tours and activities will all be done electronically and will be delivered via email and SMS.

    I haven’t received my ATX Pass via email, what should I do?

    Please reach out to support@oncethere.com or call (800) 311-ONCE (6623) to verify your email address. We’ll be able to resend your confirmation email.

    How many activities can I schedule?

    You can book as many activities as you’d like for the duration of when your ATX Pass is valid. However, you will not be able to book activities that have the same start time or overlapping duration.

    What do I present when checking in for a tour/activity?

    Upon arrival for a tour/activity, you will need to present the confirmation email for the specific tour/activity. You will not be able to check-in to the tour/activity using the confirmation email for the ATX Pass.

    I’ve entered the wrong phone number, what should I do?

    It is best to enter your mobile phone number during checkout. This phone number will be shared with the tour/activity providers in case they need to get a hold of you regarding your scheduled activities. If you have entered the wrong phone number during checkout, please reach out to the team at OnceThere by emailing support@oncethere.com.
